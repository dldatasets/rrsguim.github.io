RIDE - Representantion learning for
Interpolation, Distribution and Extrapolation
of time series by related series.


Methods to transform data frequency have application in several
areas of the economy, such as when we want a monthly series from
the quarterly GDP. In my doctoral thesis, I applied deep learning
to accomplish this task.

See technical details in Deep Learning Macroeconomics, Chapter 5.

Additionally, I developed this web page so that anyone can observe
how the proposed method works in the original data or other data.

Here is a video tutorial on how to proceed. Contact me for questions
and also to tell me the results with your data.
